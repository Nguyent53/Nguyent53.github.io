About Us

My Dream School has the Field of Dreams, a football and track field with artificial turf field. It offers sports such as basketball, hockey (not since 2005), football, softball, cricket, track and field, volleyball, badminton, cross-country.Toronto FC Juniors is a curriculum-based program specializing in grassroots development for players aged six to fourteen. With professional facilities and coaching staff Toronto FC Juniors creates a supplemental training environment.
As stated on the website, players train here once or twice per week and train and compete with their club or academy for the remainder of the week. Participation in the program automatically includes every player in the TFC Academy player pathway.
