Scholarships and Awards

The following scholarships and awards are available to students of My Dream School- St. Kim High School. Contact your school administration office for more information:
  1. Joseph Nucara Memorial Award

    2. This award has been created by the Nucara family in memory of their beloved husband and father, Joseph Nucara, who passed away in June 2016.
    Interest earned on a principal amount of $1,000, as well as any further contributions, are to be used to provide an annual $100 award to an eligible grade 9 female student from Michael Power/St. Joseph Catholic High School.

  2. Eaton-Indigenous Clean Energy Scholarship: Summer 2023

    3. Eaton Electrical Canada has proudly partnered with Indigenous Clean Energy to support Indigenous students across Canada by offering a $5,000 scholarship, along with a paid internship.

  3. De Beers Group Scholarships for Canadian Women 2023.

    4. To be eligible for an award, applicants must meet the following requirements:

    • All applicants must be Canadian citizens or have permanent residence status in Canada.
    • All applicants must be female.
    • All applicants must be entering their first year of an undergraduate program at an accredited Canadian institution.
